# 🎯 B2B Growth Scout: AI-Powered Lead Intelligence

**B2B Growth Scout** is a high-performance automated prospecting engine. Designed for agencies and sales teams that need enriched databases without wasting hours on manual searching.

## 🚀 Key Features
- **Massive Extraction:** Processes up to 500+ leads per session with intelligent auto-scroll logic.
- **Data Enrichment:** Automatically locates websites, phone numbers, and direct contact emails.
- **AI Lead Scoring:** Automatically categorizes prospects based on their digital maturity, reputation, and business potential.
- **Clean CSV Dashboard:** Generates structured reports ready to import into any CRM (Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive).
